# One-Page Buyer Memo

## AppealForge PAC

**Source-cited Medicare Advantage appeal packets for post-acute teams.**

### The problem

Post-acute teams lose time and appeal opportunities because Medicare Advantage denials require fast, evidence-heavy responses. Denial letters, therapy notes, nursing notes, discharge summaries, and payer instructions are fragmented across PDFs, portals, and EHRs.

### The product

AppealForge PAC turns a denial letter and clinical packet into:
- denial reason summary;
- appeal deadline;
- evidence map;
- missing evidence checklist;
- appeal cover letter;
- physician attestation template;
- attachment index;
- exportable packet;
- outcome tracker.

### Why it is different

- Post-acute specific.
- Appeal-first.
- Source-cited.
- Human-reviewed.
- Works from PDFs before integrations.
- Audit-ready.

### Safety

AppealForge does not make clinical decisions. Every clinical statement must be tied to a document, page, and quote. Drafts require human approval before export.

### Pilot

90-day paid pilot:
- 3-5 facilities;
- de-identified historical cases first;
- production only after compliance onboarding;
- weekly review;
- ROI dashboard.

### Metrics

- packet assembly time;
- appeal rate;
- overturn rate;
- staff time saved;
- covered days recovered;
- deadline misses avoided;
- physician review time.

### Ask

Provide 5 de-identified Medicare Advantage post-acute denial cases for a proof-of-value test.
