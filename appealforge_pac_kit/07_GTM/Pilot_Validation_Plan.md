# 14-Day Pilot Validation Plan

## Goal

Validate whether post-acute providers have frequent, painful, winnable Medicare Advantage denials and would pay for a 10-minute source-cited appeal packet workflow.

## Pass/fail criteria

Pass only if at least 5 operators say:
- MA post-acute denials recur monthly;
- appeal assembly is painful;
- some appealed denials are overturned;
- they can provide de-identified historical cases;
- an executive owns the budget;
- they would consider a paid pilot.

## Day 1-2 — Build target list

Targets:
- regional SNF operators;
- IRF operators;
- LTCH operators;
- hospital discharge leaders;
- post-acute consultants;
- billing/UR leaders.

Capture:
- organization;
- contact;
- role;
- facility count;
- MA census hypothesis;
- outreach status.

## Day 3-7 — Discovery

Ask exactly:
1. How many Medicare Advantage SNF/IRF/LTCH admission or continued-stay denials did you get last month?
2. What percentage did you appeal?
3. How long does one appeal packet take to assemble?
4. Who assembles it?
5. What documentation is hardest to find?
6. How often do denials get reversed?
7. How many admissions or covered days were lost because the appeal was too slow or not filed?
8. Can you provide 5 de-identified historical denial packets?
9. Would a 10-minute evidence-cited packet generator be worth $2k/month per facility?
10. What systems do you currently use?

## Day 8-10 — Historical packet test

Request 3-5 de-identified cases:
- denial letter;
- clinical packet;
- final appeal, if any;
- outcome.

Run manually or with prototype:
- parse denial;
- evidence map;
- missing gaps;
- draft packet;
- time measured.

## Day 11-12 — Demo back

Show:
- before/after packet time;
- evidence map;
- packet draft;
- missing evidence checklist;
- outcome tracker.

Ask:
- What would you change?
- Would staff use this?
- Would a physician trust this?
- What would block a pilot?
- Who signs off?

## Day 13-14 — Decide

Greenlight if:
- frequent denials;
- painful assembly;
- willingness to provide data;
- budget owner;
- paid pilot interest.

Pivot if:
- appeals already near 100%;
- packets take under 15 minutes;
- overturns rare;
- buyer refuses to pay;
- no access to historical cases.

## Evidence to collect

- number of denials/month;
- current appeal rate;
- current packet time;
- staff roles involved;
- appeal outcomes;
- revenue/covered days at stake;
- buyer quote;
- objections;
- pilot ask.
