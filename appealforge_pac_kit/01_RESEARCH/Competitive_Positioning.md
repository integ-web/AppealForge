# Competitive Positioning

## Category

AppealForge PAC is not a generic RCM platform, payer portal wrapper, or prior authorization clearinghouse.

It is a **post-acute denial-to-appeal evidence assembly system**.

## Existing alternatives

### 1. Manual work
Staff read denial letters, search PDFs/EHR notes, copy/paste into Word, ask nurses or therapists for missing details, assemble attachments, fax or upload manually, and track status in spreadsheets.

Weakness:
- slow;
- inconsistent;
- uncited;
- dependent on expert staff;
- high risk of missed deadlines.

### 2. Generic prior authorization tools
Useful for request submission and payer communication but often weak at post-denial clinical evidence synthesis.

Weakness:
- broad workflows;
- often integration-heavy;
- may not support appeal-specific packet generation;
- not optimized for SNF/IRF/LTCH clinical facts.

### 3. RCM suites
Good for billing workflows and claims follow-up.

Weakness:
- appeal packets for medical necessity are a specialized clinical-document task;
- users still manually build the narrative.

### 4. Outsourced appeal services
Can produce appeals but may be expensive, slow, or unavailable for every case.

Weakness:
- not instant;
- knowledge leaves the provider organization;
- hard to use for same-day admissions or urgent post-acute decisions.

### 5. Patient-side denial appeal tools
Potentially useful for consumers, but SNF admission/continued-stay appeals are provider workflow problems with clinical packet depth.

Weakness:
- not embedded in facility admissions/utilization-review workflow;
- not built for B2B packet operations.

## Differentiation

AppealForge PAC wins by being:

1. **Post-acute specific**  
   Starts with SNF/IRF/LTCH language, evidence patterns, and workflows.

2. **Appeal-first**  
   Converts a denial reason into an evidence map and packet.

3. **Source-cited**  
   Every clinical assertion links to a document, page, and quote.

4. **Human-reviewed**  
   Drafts are not submitted until approved by staff/physician.

5. **Fast without integrations**  
   PDF upload plus manual export in v1.

6. **Compliance-native**  
   RBAC, audit logs, PHI minimization, versioning, and vendor BAA readiness are built from day one.

7. **Beautiful enough to get adoption**  
   Users see a premium workflow, not another sterile portal.

## Positioning line

**AppealForge PAC is the evidence layer between a Medicare Advantage denial and a provider's reconsideration packet.**

## Do not position as

- "AI doctor"
- "medical necessity judge"
- "automated appeal sender"
- "replacement for clinical review"
- "payer hacking tool"
- "consumer claims app"
- "generic prior authorization automation"

## Position as

- denial recovery workflow;
- appeal packet assembly;
- source-cited clinical evidence mapping;
- post-acute authorization operations;
- human-reviewed medical necessity documentation support.
